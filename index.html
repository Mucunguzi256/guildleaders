WHO WE ARE

Forging Leaders for a Democratic East Africa

The Guild Presidents Leadership Academy (GPLA) is a prestigious regional institution based at Makerere University, Uganda — the birthplace of African student activism.

We've convened over 700 youth leaders from 6 countries, hosting sitting Vice Presidents, former Heads of State, EU Ambassadors, and Electoral Commission Chairpersons.

Our Programs

BUILD YOUR LEADERSHIP TOOLKIT

Transformational Leadership Bootcamp (TLB)

5 Days Residential Intensive

A rigorous 5-day residential program building essential leadership skills for effective student governance. Participants engage in strategic planning, public speaking, crisis management, and team dynamics training.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Master strategic planning and decision-making frameworks
  • ✓ Develop crisis management and conflict resolution skills
  • ✓ Build a lifelong network of 100+ fellow guild leaders
Learn More →

Policy & Governance Incubation Lab (PGIL)

8 Weeks Hybrid Research-Focused

8-week intensive program building practical policy analysis and advocacy skills. Fellows work on real policy challenges facing East Africa, producing actionable research for regional institutions.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Produce a publishable policy brief on a regional issue
  • ✓ Present research to senior policymakers and diplomats
  • ✓ Join the alumni network of policy practitioners
Learn More →

HERPower Fellowship

6 Months Mentorship Women Only

Specialized fellowship for young women passionate about political leadership. HERPower provides mentorship, networking, and capacity building to increase women's representation in student governance and beyond.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ 1-on-1 mentorship from established female leaders
  • ✓ Leadership coaching and personal branding training
  • ✓ Access to HERPower alumnae network of 200+ women
Learn More →

Guild Leaders' Summit + Guild Voice

2 Days Annual 700+ Attendees

Annual flagship gathering bringing together Guild Presidents from across East Africa. The Summit features high-level plenary sessions, workshops, and the Guild Voice policy platform where youth voices are amplified.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Address heads of state, ministers, and regional leaders
  • ✓ Publish the Guild Voice Declaration for EAC policymakers
  • ✓ Build cross-border networks with 700+ peer leaders
