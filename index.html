WHO WE ARE

Forging Leaders for a Democratic East Africa

The Guild Presidents Leadership Academy (GPLA) is a prestigious regional institution based at Makerere University, Uganda — the birthplace of African student activism.

We've convened over 700 youth leaders from 6 countries, hosting sitting Vice Presidents, former Heads of State, EU Ambassadors, and Electoral Commission Chairpersons.

Our Programs

BUILD YOUR LEADERSHIP TOOLKIT

5 Days · Residential

Transformational Leadership Bootcamp (TLB)

A rigorous 5-day residential program building essential leadership skills for effective student governance.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Personal leadership vision creation with actionable steps
  • ✓ Conflict resolution and strategic planning skills
  • ✓ Mentorship with public, private, and civil society leaders

8 Weeks · Hybrid

Policy & Governance Incubation Lab (PGIL)

8-week intensive program building practical policy analysis and advocacy skills for East Africa.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Public policy analysis and budgeting competency
  • ✓ Real-time review of national development plans
  • ✓ Policy briefs and campus-based governance initiatives
Apply for PGIL →
Ongoing · Mentorship

HERPower Fellowship

Specialized fellowship for young women passionate about political leadership.

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Feminist leadership and political organizing skills
  • ✓ Mentorship from women leaders in public office
  • ✓ Network of women committed to democratic leadership
