ABOUT THE SUMMIT

The Guild Leaders' Annual Summit is GPLA's flagship event, bringing together over 700 student leaders from across East Africa. Held annually at Makerere University, the summit serves as a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and capacity building for the next generation of African leaders.
