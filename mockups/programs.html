OUR PROGRAMS

01
Leadership & Development

Transformational Leadership Bootcamp

A rigorous 5-day residential program that equips Guild Presidents with essential leadership skills for effective governance. Participants engage with world-class facilitators from public, private, and civil society sectors.

  • Personal Leadership Vision Development
  • Conflict Resolution & Mediation
  • Strategic Planning & Execution
  • Mentorship with Senior Leaders
  • Public Speaking & Communication
02
Governance & Policy

Policy & Governance Incubation Lab

An 8-week intensive program designed to build practical policy analysis and advocacy skills. Fellows work on real governance challenges facing East African communities.

  • Policy Analysis Methods
  • Legislative Process Understanding
  • Budget Analysis & Monitoring
  • Evidence-Based Advocacy
  • Civic Engagement Strategies
03
Women in Leadership

HERPower Fellowship

A specialized fellowship for young women passionate about political leadership and civic engagement. The program addresses the unique challenges women face in East African politics.

  • Political Campaign Management
  • Gender & Policy Analysis
  • Network Building with Women Leaders
  • Candidate Training
  • Media & Communications
04
Regional Convening

Guild Leaders' Summit & Guild Voice Initiative

Our flagship annual gathering brings together Guild Presidents from across East Africa to discuss regional challenges, share best practices, and build lasting networks.

  • Annual Summit at Makerere University
  • Cross-Border Networking
  • Regional Policy Dialogues
  • Alumni Engagement Platform
  • Media Training for Leaders
