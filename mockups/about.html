WHO WE ARE

Rooted in Makerere. Built for East Africa.

VISION

Empowering ethical youth leaders for democratic transformation in East Africa.

MISSION

To equip outgoing student leaders with the knowledge, skills, and values to influence governance and public policy across East Africa.

OBJECTIVES

1. Build leadership capacity
2. Promote democratic values
3. Foster policy engagement
4. Enable youth voice
5. Create regional networks
6. Drive civic participation

OUR STORY

From a Vision to a Regional Movement

The Guild Presidents Leadership Academy (GPLA) was established as a flagship initiative to bridge the gap between student leadership and effective governance across East Africa.

Based at Makerere University, the birthplace of African student activism, GPLA equips outgoing Guild Presidents with the knowledge, values, and networks needed to transform governance across the region.

Since our founding, we have convened over 700 youth leaders from 6 countries, hosted sitting Vice Presidents, former Heads of State, EU Ambassadors, and Electoral Commission Chairpersons.
