Our Story

The Guild Presidents Leadership Academy (GPLA) is a prestigious regional institution based at Makerere University, Uganda — widely recognized as the birthplace of African student activism.

Since our founding, we've convened over 700 youth leaders from 6 countries across East Africa. Our programs have hosted sitting Vice Presidents, former Heads of State, EU Ambassadors, and Electoral Commission Chairpersons as speakers and mentors.

We believe that outgoing Guild Presidents represent a untapped reservoir of leadership talent. By providing them with the knowledge, values, and networks needed for effective governance, we're building the next generation of East Africa's democratic leaders.

"History has been made."

— Demiano Masesa
Executive Director, GPLA

OUR MISSION

To equip outgoing Guild Presidents with the knowledge, values, and professional networks required to drive democratic transformation across East Africa.

OUR VISION

A unified East Africa where ethical youth leadership thrives through strong democratic institutions and active citizen participation.
